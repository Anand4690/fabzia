Frequently Asked Questions

Browse available turfs on the Home screen, select your preferred turf, choose your date, time slot and squad size, then proceed to payment. You'll receive a booking confirmation once payment is successful.

Go to your Bookings page, select the booking you wish to cancel, and tap "Cancel Booking". Cancellations made at least 2 hours before the slot time are eligible for a full refund to your Fabzia Wallet.

Refunds are processed to your Fabzia Wallet within 24 hours of a successful cancellation. You can use your wallet balance for future bookings or request a bank transfer from the Wallet page.

Currently, rescheduling is not supported directly. You can cancel your existing booking and create a new one with your preferred date and time. The refund from the cancelled booking will be available in your wallet.

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and Fabzia Wallet balance. You can also combine wallet balance with other payment methods during checkout.

If you face any issues with the turf condition or facilities, please contact our support team via email or phone within 24 hours of your booking. We'll investigate and take appropriate action.
